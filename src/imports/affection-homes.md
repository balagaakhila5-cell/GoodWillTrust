Affection Homes


“बालं शरणं गच्छति सर्वजनं तु साधयेत्।
“Let all beings strive to support the child who seeks refuge.”

“Who will call them orphans when all of us are here to care for them?”

Our journey with Seva Bharathi shows that every child deserves a loving, nurturing home. Our orphanage homes, called Awasam, care for boys and girls who have lost their families or need a safe place. These homes are not just shelters. They are hopeful places where children can thrive. Our 15 centers have helped 573 beneficiaries, thanks to our 45 volunteers. We give each child the love and guidance they deserve. No one should ever feel like an orphan in our care.

Join us in making a difference in the lives of these children. Your support can help us continue this vital work.

Vanavasi Vikasa Kendram [Boys]
