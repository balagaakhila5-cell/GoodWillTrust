About Goodwill Trust
Goodwill Trust was founded with a simple yet powerful belief — every individual deserves dignity, opportunity, and the chance to build their own future.
In many parts of society, persons with disabilities are often seen through the lens of limitation. Opportunities are restricted, talents remain unnoticed, and dreams are left unexplored. At Goodwill Trust, we believe the opposite — disability is not inability; it is a different ability waiting to be empowered.
Our mission is to transform lives by empowering persons with disabilities to become confident entrepreneurs, skilled professionals, and self-reliant individuals. We envision a society where people with disabilities are not dependent on charity, but are respected contributors to the economy and community.
Our Vision
To build an inclusive society where persons with disabilities are empowered, independent, and recognized as capable entrepreneurs and leaders.
Our Mission
Our mission is to identify, train, mentor, and support persons with disabilities so they can develop sustainable livelihoods and become successful entrepreneurs.
We aim to create opportunities that enable them to earn with dignity, lead with confidence, and inspire others through their achievements.
What We Do
At Goodwill Trust, we work at the grassroots level to create meaningful change through innovative initiatives and community participation.
1. Entrepreneurship Development
We provide training, guidance, and mentorship to persons with disabilities to help them start and manage their own businesses.
2. Skill Development Programs
We conduct workshops and training sessions that help individuals learn practical skills that can generate income and build self-confidence.
3. Goodwill Stores Initiative
Inspired by global social enterprise models, we aim to create Goodwill Stores where donated or unused materials are collected, recycled, and converted into useful products. These stores will serve as both employment hubs and entrepreneurial platforms for persons with disabilities and women entrepreneurs.
4. Recycling for Social Impact
Through our recycling initiatives, unused materials are transformed into useful products, creating employment opportunities while also contributing to environmental sustainability.
5. Community Support & Awareness
We actively work to build awareness in society about the capabilities of persons with disabilities and encourage communities to support inclusive development.
Our Philosophy
We believe that true empowerment happens when people are given opportunity, not sympathy.
Instead of offering temporary help, we focus on creating sustainable livelihoods that allow individuals to stand on their own feet and support their families with pride.
Every person we support is not just a beneficiary — they are a partner in building a stronger and more inclusive society.
Why Goodwill Trust?
Goodwill Trust is more than an organization.
It is a movement of hope, dignity, and opportunity.
Our approach combines social impact, entrepreneurship, and community collaboration to build long-term solutions for persons with disabilities.
We believe that when the right support system is created, talent can rise above every limitation.
Our Commitment
We are committed to:
• Empowering persons with disabilities through entrepreneurship
• Creating sustainable employment opportunities
• Promoting dignity, independence, and self-confidence
• Building an inclusive ecosystem where everyone has equal opportunity to succeed
Join the Movement
The journey towards empowerment cannot be done alone. It requires collective effort, compassion, and collaboration.
We invite individuals, organizations, businesses, and communities to join hands with Goodwill Trust in creating opportunities for persons with disabilities.
Together, we can build a future where ability is celebrated, opportunities are equal, and every dream gets a chance to become reality.
